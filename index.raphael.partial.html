Raphael

“The Transfiguration”

This painting is full of symbolism. Some people believe that the painting represents the different aspects of Jesus’ nature, both human and divine. Others believe that the painting is an allegory of the soul’s journey to enlightenment.

Bartolomé Esteban Murillo

“Virgin and Child”

Murillo’s paintings of the Virgin and Child are popular because he imbues the traditional theme with a sense of intimacy and sweetness. This is evident in the soft modeling of the figures, as well as in the infant’s momentary glance away from the Virgin.
