Art is Eternal

Model by Frederick Sandys

This permanent exhibition at RenArt gallery explores the development of Renaissance art over time. The exhibition features works from the early Renaissance to its latter stages and contemporary attempts by artists to copy the style.

Embrace the Renaissance's Timeless Splendour :Where Art Transcends and Inspires

We are thrilled to present "Art Beyond Time," an extraordinary exhibition that breathes new life into the timeless beauty of Renaissance art. This innovative showcase challenges traditional norms and invites viewers to explore the dynamic connections between the past and the present.

In this exhibition, you will encounter thought-provoking adaptations of Renaissance masterpieces alongside modern interpretations inspired by the same sense of balance, proportion, and technique. The artworks engage in a captivating dialogue, inviting viewers to reflect on contemporary artistic influence of the Renaissance on the present day.
