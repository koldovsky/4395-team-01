Frans van Mieris (II) "The Grocer’s Shop"

Following the footsteps of the painter's ancestors

This painting, The Grocer's Shop, can serve as a pendant to The Poultry Seller, which was painted by the father of Frans van Mieris the Younger and is also on display. The egg basket at the upper right appears to have been passed down through the generations. "The Grocer’s Shop" by Frans van Mieris the Younger
