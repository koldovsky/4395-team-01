You Ask and RenArt Gallery Answers

If you would like to know more about visiting our gallery or about the Renaissance, check out this section of our site. Also, you can always contact our employees with a question in any way convenient for you.

How can I buy tickets?
Due to too high demand, we do not sell tickets directly on the site. Fill out the form, and we will contact you to confirm your order.
Are guided tours available?
Yes, guided tours are available every Friday and Saturday. Please contact us in advance to reserve a spot.
Do you have parking facilities?
There is a small parking area near the main entrance. Additional parking is available on nearby streets.
Can I bring my bag or backpack?
Large bags and backpacks are not allowed inside the gallery. You can leave them in our cloakroom free of charge.
What are current exhibitions?
Our permanent exhibition “Art Beyond Time” is currently open. Check our events page for upcoming temporary exhibitions.
